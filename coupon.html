What is the Referral System?

It's a very simple system that allows you to earn money from purchases made by your friends.

How does it work?

- You ask for your unique discount code.
- You advertise us to your friends with your code.
- You receive a flat 10% from every sale made with your code.
- For example, if your friend buys something for 10€ using your code, you receive 1€ to your balance.

What's the Percentage Discount for My Code?

The code gives a 15% discount for every product on our website.

What's the minimum amount for withdrawing my funds?

To withdraw your balance, you need to accumulate at least 10€.

What Are the Available Payout Methods?

Available payout methods are: Paypal, Skrill, Cryptocurrency.

How can I start?

To start your Referral System, simply create a ticket in our Discord server. We are going to create your balance and unique code within minutes.
